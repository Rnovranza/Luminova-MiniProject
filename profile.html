What is Luminova?

Leminova is a property and food company that is dedicated to creating high-quality products and services for its customers. With a focus on innovation, quality, and sustainability, Leminova aims to provide its customers with the best possible experience in both the property and food industries.


In the property industry, Leminova offers a range of products and services, including commercial and residential properties, property management, and construction services. Leminova's focus on innovation and sustainability is reflected in its property development, with a commitment to green building practices and sustainable design. With a team of experienced professionals, Leminova is dedicated to providing its clients with exceptional service, and creating properties that meet the highest standards of quality and design.


In the food industry, Leminova offers a range of products and services, including catering services, food production, and restaurant management. With a focus on quality and innovation, Leminova is committed to using the highest quality ingredients and providing its customers with the best possible dining experience. Whether it's catering for a special event, producing high-quality food products, or managing a restaurant, Leminova's team of experienced professionals is dedicated to providing its customers with exceptional service and quality. With a commitment to sustainability and responsible sourcing, Leminova is a leader in the food industry, and is dedicated to providing its customers with the best possible products and services.

Vision

To be the leading property and food company in the region, committed to providing innovative, sustainable, and high-quality products and services that exceed customer expectations.

Mision

  1. To provide our customers with exceptional property and food products and services that meet the highest standards of quality and design.
  2. To continuously innovate and improve our products and services to meet the changing needs of our customers.
  3. To operate in a sustainable and responsible manner, with a focus on green building practices and responsible sourcing of ingredients.
  4. To foster a culture of collaboration, respect, and excellence, with a dedicated team of professionals who are committed to delivering exceptional service to our customers.
  5. To create long-term value for our shareholders, by delivering sustainable growth and profitability through our property and food businesses.
